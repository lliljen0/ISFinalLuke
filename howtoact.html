How to ACT while Golfing

Here are some good one-liners you should keep in your backpocket for when you're playing with friends:

  • When your buddy misses a putt you say: Right club though
  • When your buddy shanks it you say: Outside of your speed, direction, and contact, it was a great shot.
  • If your buddy ever misses the ball you say: Man, you couldn't hit water if you fell out of a boat.
  • Find every opportunity to sing and dance to Zac Efron's "Bet On It" from High School Musical 2.
